About Me

Business Digital Student

Hi! I’m Adinda Amirah Dahri,just call me Dinda. I’m a 19-year-old Digital Business undergraduate at Makassar State University, born and raised in Palopo City. I find joy in the quiet things: singing, watching films, playing with cats, and browsing for simple sparks of happiness. As an introvert, I tend to be shy around new people, but I’ve learned to push past that when needed and grow from every experience.

I’m currently exploring the worlds of UI/UX design, web development, and digital marketing through small projects and self-learning. I’m also building a foundation in data analysis and business strategy skills I believe are key to my future goals. I value responsibility, collaboration, and lifelong learning.

My Learning Journey

From MTsN in Palopo City, continued to IT High School Daarul Mushaf lil Fatayaat, then briefly boarded at Modern Pesantren Datok Sulaiman... and now I continue my studies at Makassar State University.
Every place has its own stories and lessons. Not just about academics, but also about growing up, getting to know yourself, and learning to be a better version of yourself.
