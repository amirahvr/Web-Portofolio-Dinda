About Me

Business Digital Student

Hi! I’m Adinda Amirah Dahri, or simply Dinda. I’m a 19-year-old Digital Business undergraduate student at Makassar State University, originally from Palopo City. I'm someone who enjoys quiet moments—singing, watching films, playing with cats, and browsing for little things that spark joy. Although I’m naturally introverted and often shy around new people, I’ve learned to step out of my comfort zone when needed. I value responsibility, teamwork, and am always eager to learn and grow through every experience life offers.

Right now, I’m diving into the world of UI/UX design, web development, and digital marketing, exploring these fields through small projects and continuous learning. I’m also building skills in data analysis and business strategy—tools I believe are essential for my future career. I see myself as someone who’s passionate, driven, and always curious. Through this blog, I hope to share parts of my journey, thoughts, and lessons along the way—with a little heart and a lot of learning.

My Learning Journey

From MTsN in Palopo City, continued to IT High School Daarul Mushaf lil Fatayaat, then briefly boarded at Modern Pesantren Datok Sulaiman... and now I continue my studies at Makassar State University.
Every place has its own stories and lessons. Not just about academics, but also about growing up, getting to know yourself, and learning to be a better version of yourself.
